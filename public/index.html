The promise

Imagine an associate who has read every page.

One who flags the privilege you might miss. Surfaces the contradiction buried four hundred pages deep. Shows you exactly why every recommendation was made — and waits for your call before doing anything about it.

MerakAi isn't here to replace your judgment. It's here to give you more time to use it.

How MerakAi works

From first upload to final response, the platform reads alongside you.

A litigator's workflow, made faster — not replaced. Five stages, each one transparent and reversible.

01

Ingest the matter

Drop documents, emails, productions, transcripts. MerakAi extracts metadata, builds a custodian map, and starts reading immediately. No batch jobs to schedule. No load files to wrestle.

Common formats supported out of the box. Extracted text, threading, and conversation reconstruction happen in the background.

02

Read every page

MerakAi reads every document for substance — not just keywords. It surfaces themes, contradictions, key custodians, and timeline anchors. Privilege candidates are flagged with reasoning. Concept clusters emerge automatically.

You get a map of your document set before you've reviewed a single one. The map sharpens as you work.

03

Frame the case

A guided walkthrough of your case theory: claims, defenses, key dates, custodians, what you need to prove, what you need to find. MerakAi turns it into a living research plan that runs against your document set continuously.

As facts develop, your theory updates. The platform re-evaluates findings against the new frame automatically.

04

Review intelligently

AI suggestions arrive pre-coded. Reviewers see them next to a blank human field — accept, reject, or refine in one click. Every override teaches the platform. Bulk actions show previews before they commit. Search understands meaning, not just words.

The full toolkit is here — Boolean, concept search, regex, KWIC, saved searches, mass tagging — but applied through reasoning, not rote.

05

Build the response

Production-ready output with full audit trail. Every coding decision, every override, every bulk action attributed and timestamped. Defensible at deposition. Defensible at hearing. Defensible at trial.

Your work product, ready to ship. The reasoning behind every call, ready to defend.

The platform

Three things change when your platform reads the case.

Phase 1 — Before review

Most platforms wait for you. MerakAi shows up early.

AI Case Theory

Tell us your case. We'll build the strategy.

Upload a complaint — or answer 10 guided questions if you don't have one. MerakAi extracts every party, claim, cause of action, and damages figure, then builds a case theory tailored to which side you represent. That theory drives every AI decision downstream. Update as the case evolves; the platform re-reads everything against the new frame.

AI Evidence Mapping

Know what's missing before opposing counsel does.

Most platforms only show you what you've loaded. MerakAi maps gaps in your collection, identifies custodians you haven't pulled from, and flags spoliation risks before they become problems. What's missing matters as much as what's there.

Phase 2 — During review

Review decisions, not documents.

AI Privilege Detection

Stop missing privileged documents in keyword searches.

Every document is read for the substance of attorney-client communication, work product, and common interest privilege — not just the words "attorney" or "confidential." Each flag comes with a confidence level and the reasoning behind it. You confirm, override, or refine. Every call is logged.

Two-Tier Coding

AI suggests. You decide. Side by side.

Every document arrives with an AI recommendation already in place — responsiveness, privilege, issue tags, confidentiality. Your reviewers see the suggestion next to a blank human field. Accept, reject, or refine in one click. When you reject, MerakAi asks why — and learns. Ten times the speed without ceding a single decision to the machine.

Phase 3 — When the response is due

Three days of work in three minutes.

AI Response Drafting

Paste their requests. We draft the responses.

Paste opposing counsel's RFPs. MerakAi parses every request, drafts responses with appropriate legal objections, links the responsive documents to each one, and generates a filing-ready response. The single biggest time-sink in document discovery, collapsed.

Ask MerakAi

Ask any question. Get a real answer.

"What custodians have we collected from?" "Show me every document that contradicts their answer to RFP 7." Plain-English questions, real answers — not keyword hits. The platform knows your case because it's been reading it the whole time.
